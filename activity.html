Make Something with Webmaker

Make something with Webmaker!

  1. On an Android phone, download and install Webmaker.

    To install Webmaker at the moment, you may need to enable "Unkown sources" in your app security settings.
    Go to Settings > Security > Unknown sources.

  2. After installing, open the Webmaker app and explore the projects people have made.
  3. Click on the "Make" tab and start a new project.
  4. Make something! Check out this list for inspiration.
  5. Give your project a title and share it with @webmaker or bobby@mozillafoundation.org.
